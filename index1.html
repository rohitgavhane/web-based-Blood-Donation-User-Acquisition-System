Blood Donation

Blood donation is the process of voluntarily giving blood to a blood bank or collection center. Donated blood is used to save lives in emergency situations, surgeries, cancer treatments, and other medical procedures. .

Blood Donation Steps

The donation process from the time you arrive center until the time you leave

Registration

You need to complte a very simple regulation form. Which contains all required contact information to enter in the donation process.

Doctors Advice

You need to complte a simple and supportive Doctor's advise before and after blood donation contains all required to enter in the donation process.

Donation

After ensuring and passed test successfully you will be directed to a donar bed for donation. It will take only 6-10 minutes.
